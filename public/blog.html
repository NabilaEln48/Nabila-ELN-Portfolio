ABOUT ME

Hey, I’m Nabila “ELN” — this little corner of the internet is where I collect my favorite things: drawings, photos, and the occasional code project that sneaks in. I’m inspired by clean design, bold ideas, and the magic of turning random sparks of inspiration into something you can see or use.
Most days you’ll find me experimenting with colors, snapping photos, or getting lost in a sketch. Other days, I’m chasing waves, learning new tricks on a skateboard, or starting a new hobby just to see where it takes me. This blog is my space to share those adventures, big or small.
